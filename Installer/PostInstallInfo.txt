NEXT STEPS:
===========

STEP 1: LAUNCH THE APPLICATION
-------------------------------
Click "Finish" to launch ChronoPos Desktop now, or launch it later using:
- Desktop shortcut: "ChronoPos Desktop"
- Start menu: Programs > ChronoPos Desktop

STEP 2: COMPLETE LICENSE ACTIVATION
------------------------------------
On first launch, you'll need to:
1. Enter your Sales Key (provided by ChronoPos)
2. Fill in business information:
   - Business Name
   - Contact Person
   - Phone Number
   - Email Address
   - Business Address
3. Scan or upload your business document for verification

STEP 3: CREATE ADMIN ACCOUNT
-----------------------------
Set up your administrator account:
- Choose a strong username
- Create a secure password
- This account will have full system access

STEP 4: CONFIGURE YOUR BUSINESS
--------------------------------
After initial setup, configure:
1. Business Information
   - Tax registration details
   - Currency and tax rates
   - Business hours

2. Product Catalog
   - Add product categories
   - Import or add products
   - Set up pricing and inventory

3. Team Setup
   - Create employee accounts
   - Assign roles and permissions
   - Configure shift management

4. Hardware Setup (Optional)
   - Connect receipt printer
   - Configure barcode scanner
   - Set up cash drawer

TROUBLESHOOTING:
================

If you encounter any issues:

1. Check application logs:
   %LocalAppData%\ChronoPos\app.log

2. Verify database exists:
   %LocalAppData%\ChronoPos\chronopos.db

3. Ensure proper permissions:
   - Run as Administrator if needed

4. Contact support:
   - Provide error messages
   - Include log files
   - Describe steps to reproduce

UNINSTALLATION:
===============

To uninstall ChronoPos Desktop:
1. Go to: Settings > Apps > Installed Apps
2. Find "ChronoPos Desktop"
3. Click Uninstall

During uninstallation, you'll be asked:
- Keep or delete application data?
- Keep your data to reinstall later
- Delete to completely remove everything
